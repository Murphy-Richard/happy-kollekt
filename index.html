C Participant Consent

HAPPY Program collects participant data to support training, placement, reporting, and program improvement. Consent is required before registration.

Purpose

We collect personal data to analyse program progress, improve services, and monitor the effectiveness of the Harnessing Agricultural Productivity and Prosperity for Youth program.

Data Collected

  • Name, age, gender, contact details, education, location, participation details, CV status, and employability information.
  • Refugee/displaced status, disability status, training details, and job placement details where applicable.

Data Sharing

Data may be shared with Jobberman Ghana, Agri-Impact Limited, funding partners, and approved data processors for monitoring, evaluation, and program delivery.
